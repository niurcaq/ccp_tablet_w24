My Heritage

My parents were born in Mexico. So I'm proud to be a Mexican-American! I absolutely adore Mexico and would recommend anyone to go and visit. Mexico has some of the best hospitatlity from anyone. Not only does Mexico have beautiful weather, it has exquisite food, and stunning views. Some places I would recommend to visit are Cancun, Puerto Vallarta, Mexico City, and Guadalajara.

Cancun and Puerto Vallarta are some of the most common tourist spots with beautiful beaches and views that can't be explained. Mexico city, La cuidad de Mexico, is the capital of Mexico. It's a great place to visit and learn more about Mexico! Guadalajara is one of the best cities to visit in Jalisco, other than Puerto Vallarta. It has beautiful farm lands, mountain views, and a huge elegant mall. Mexico is a great place to vacation as the US dollar is worth about 18 pesos in Mexico. As refernce, my family is made up of 7 people, so a family takeout meal can go anywhere from eighty five to a hundred twenty dollars. However, in Mexico I was able to buy my entire family a fulfilling meal and dessert for twenty five dollars.
